What Is Included In A Free Roof Estimate?


A free roof estimate is an estimate of the cost to install a new roof, perform roof repairs to an existing roof, or a full roof replacement of an entire roof.

Roofing estimates can include a variety of different factors. The estimate will vary depending on the type of roofing material, the size and shape of the roof repair, and desired features like vents and skylights. The roofing materials needed depend on the type of roof replacement you want to install. Clay or concrete tiles are more expensive than asphalt shingles but they last longer and can be placed in more locations such as sloped roofs or near walkways where there is risk for heavy foot traffic. If you want metal sheeting instead, it will depend on how much coverage you need for your roofing project as well as what kind of metal sheeting you choose (low-slope metal versus standing seam metal). Factors like pitch can also affect roofing construction costs as well because steeper pitches require more material to cover them adequately. A professional roofing estimator will ask questions about your desired features before estimating your final cost but most homeowners and business owners get roof replacement estimates for just a basic installation that includes asphalt shingle installation with eaves troughs gutters only at one end to keep water from seeping under (called a “full-eave” installation). You may have any combination of gutters or downspouts installed with your estimate depending on where they are located and what type they are.
